About Hunt: Showdown 1896

The Vision: High Stakes, Higher Tension

At Crytek, we have always pushed the boundaries of what first-person shooters can be. With Hunt: Showdown 1896, we didn't just want to create another competitive multiplayer game—we set out to craft an unforgiving, atmospheric experience where every sound matters, every bullet counts, and the tension never lets up.
We combine the desperation of survival horror with the thrill of match-based PvPvE (Player vs Player vs Environment) gameplay, dropping players into a world where the environment is just as lethal as the rival Hunters tracking them.

Core Pillars of the Hunt

Supernatural PvPvE Extraction

Players step into the boots of desperate Hunters tracking down monstrous targets for bounty. You investigate clues, banish gruesome bosses, and attempt to escape with the prize before other players take it—and your life—from you.

Masterful Audio Design

Sound is gameplay. Our advanced spatial audio engine lets you pinpoint enemies by the snap of a twig, the splash of a swamp, or the distant call of a startled crow. In the Bayou, listening is your greatest weapon.

Powered by CRYENGINE

Leveraging our proprietary game engine, Hunt: Showdown 1896 delivers stunning visual fidelity, realistic physics, and immersive lighting that breathes terrifying life into every shadow of Mammon's Gulch and the Louisiana Swamps.

Evolution to 1896

What started as a cult hit has evolved into a massive global phenomenon. The transition to Hunt: Showdown 1896 represents a monumental leap forward for our team:

Next-Gen Engine Upgrade: Full integration of CRYENGINE 5.11, bringing vastly improved performance, lighting, and audio fidelity.

A New Frontier: Moving beyond the bayou to introduce the rugged, vertical terrain of Colorado's Mammon's Gulch.

Deepened Progression: A completely overhauled UI and deeper recruitment systems designed to immerse players fully in the Hunter lifestyle.

"We don't make games where you feel safe. We make games where survival feels like an achievement."

— The Crytek Development Team
