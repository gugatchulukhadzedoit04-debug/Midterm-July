FAQ & Contact

How and where can I buy and install Crytek games?

Crytek titles like Hunt: Showdown 1896 are completely digital. On PC, you can purchase, download, and launch games through Steam. For console players, the games are available digitally via the PlayStation Store (for PlayStation 5) and the Microsoft Store (for Xbox Series X/S). Simply purchase the game on your platform's store, click "Install," and the platform client will automatically handle the download and setup.

What is the return policy for games and physical merchandise?

For digital game purchases, refunds are subject to the platform policy where you bought the game (e.g., Steam's 2-hour playtime / 14-day ownership rule). For official physical merchandise, unused and undamaged items in their original packaging can be returned within 30 days of the purchase date for a refund or product exchange.

How is merchandise delivery handled and who tracks my package?

Crytek partners with specialized e-commerce and manufacturing fulfillment platforms to handle physical goods. Official apparel and accessories are primarily fulfilled through Amazon, while high-quality metal art prints are manufactured and shipped by Displate. When you order merchandise, your shipping updates, carrier tracking codes, and delivery logistics are managed directly by those sponsored companies.

What are the requirements to join the Crytek Partner Program?

Crytek has a formal Creator Program for streamers, video creators, and community leaders. To be accepted, you must meet specific baseline criteria: streaming or publishing content consistently, maintaining a minimum baseline of active, organic viewership (typically evaluated across Twitch or YouTube metrics over a 30-day period), and keeping your community free of toxic behavior or terms of service violations

I have a specific technical or billing problem. how do I contact support?

If you encounter account issues, payment errors, or major technical glitches, you should visit the official Crytek Support Portal to submit a help ticket. For direct correspondence regarding general inquiries, community issues, or account safety, you can reach out to their specialized team via email at contact@crytek.com
